The Kairos School of Computing was started in 2014. Our goal is to help students learn computing and become skilled professionals.When the school first started, we had only a few classes and a small group of students. As more people became interested in computers,our school grew bigger. We slowly added more subjects and more teachers. Now we have Foundation, Diploma, and Undergraduate programmes, so students can choose the programmes based on interest them.We try our best to give students a good place to study and learn new skills.



Vision & Mission



Our Vision

Our vission is to make Kairos School of Computing to become a good school for learning computing.

We want our students can use what they learn to do useful things in technology and help others.


Our Mission

Our mission is provide good education in computing.

We want to help students learn important skills, solve problems, and get hands-on experience.


Our Faculty

Our lectures are experienced in computer science, AI & Robotics.

They focus on practical lessons and support students in their studies and projects.


Campus and Facilities

Our campus has modern computer labs and updated equipment. Students can use these facilities for coding, research, and group projects. We also provide comfortable study areas where students can work together. With a well-prepared and safe environment, students can learn better and enjoy their time on campus.
