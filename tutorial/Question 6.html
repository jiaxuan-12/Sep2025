Chicken Curry





Ingredients:
  • 800g chicken
  • 1/2-1 coconut milk
  • 180g curry paste
  • 3 large potato
  • 3 large onion
  • 3 stalks curry leaves
Steps:

  1. Saute curry paste hot oil until fragrant.
    • Add in chicken.
    • Continue to saute until chicken releases oil.
  2. Add in water, tamarind paste, curry leaves, potato, onion, sugar and salt.
  3. Let chicken simmer in slow fire until potatoes becomes soft ansd chicken is cooked thoroughly.
  4. add in coconut milk and cook until oil starts to separate.
  5. Remove and serve.
